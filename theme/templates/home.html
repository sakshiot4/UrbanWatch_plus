UrbanWatch+ is a government-backed initiative. Reporting issues is 100% free for citizens. The repairs are funded by your local municipal corporation's maintenance budget.

We handle civic infrastructure maintenance. You can report:

  • > Road Issues (Potholes, Damaged Dividers)
  • > Sanitation (Uncollected Garbage, Dead Animals)
  • > Electrical (Broken Streetlights, Exposed Wires)
  • > Water (Pipeline Leaks, Clogged Drains)

Timelines depend on the category. Simple issues (like garbage) have a 24-hour SLA. Complex infrastructure repairs (like roadwork) typically take 3 to 7 days.
